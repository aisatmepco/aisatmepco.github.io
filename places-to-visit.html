Hello, welcome to Virudhunagar!

Virudhunagar district is leading in the country in the match industry, Fireworks and Printing, mostly concentrated in and around Sivakasi. Virudhunagar is the main market for oil, chicory, coffee seeds, dry chillies and pulses. There are two trade centres (Godowns) in the District one in Virudhunagar and another in Rajapalayam.

Ginning factories, spinning mills, power loom and hand loom industries are also present in the district at Rajapalayam the second big weaving town in the state after Coimbatore. One of the cement plants of Madras Cements Ltd is located in RR Nagar near Viudhunagar another one located at Alankulam of Sivakasi Taluk.

Popular nearby destinations



Meenakshi Amman Temple, Madurai

Arulmigu Meenakshi Amman Temple, also known as Meenakshi Sundareshwarar Temple, is a historic Hindu temple located on the southern bank of the Vaigai River in the temple city of Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Srivilliputtur Arulmigu Nachiyar (Andal) Sametha Rangamannar Temple

Srivilliputhur Andal Temple in Srivilliputhur, a town in Virudhunagar district in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu, is dedicated to the Hindu god Vishnu. It is located 80 km from Madurai.

Visit now
Shenbaga Thoppu

Shenbaga Thoppu Meenvetti Parai Falls is found in Shenbaga Thoppu, a timberland territory on the eastern slants of the Western Ghats. ‘Thoppu’ in Tamil signifies ‘forests’, and Shenbaga Thoppu is one of the most old regular forests of Tamilnadu. The woods is a ways off of around 8 kms from Srivilliputhur in Virudhunagar District.

Visit now
Courtallam

Courtallam is a panchayat town situated at a mean elevation of 160 m in the foothills of the Western Ghats in Tenkasi district of Tamil Nadu, India. The Coutrallam Falls on the Chittar River is a major tourist attraction.
