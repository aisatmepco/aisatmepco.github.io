Prof. Arvind Ayyer

Arvind Ayyer, works as Associate Professor Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. His areas of specialization are Probability Theory, Combinatorics, Statistical Physics, Mathematical Physics and Experimental Mathematics.

Topics for discussion:

  • Ordinary and Exponential Generating functions, Recurrences and ogfs for combinatorial numbers in Module 1. Exponential formula for egfs and uses in enumerating polynomials (like Eulerian polynomial), Compositional formula for egfs and its permutation version.
